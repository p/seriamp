  • VOL
    [PUT Command]
    @ZONE2:VOL=Parameter
    [GET Command]
    @ZONE2:VOL=?
    Operating & retrieving Zone 2 Volume state
    Initial Auto Feedback is Available
    [Parameters]
    Down
    PUT Only: decrementing by 1 step (0.5dB)
    Up
    PUT Only: incrementing by 1 step (0.5dB)
    Down 1 dB
    PUT Only: decrementing by 1 dB
    Up 1 dB
    PUT Only: incrementing by 1 dB
    Down 2 dB
    PUT Only: decrementing by 2 dB
    Up 2 dB
    PUT Only: incrementing by 2 dB
    Down 5 dB
    PUT Only: decrementing by 5 dB
    Up 5 dB
    PUT Only: incrementing by 5 dB
    Numerical Number: -80.5 to 16.5, Step: 0.5
    PUT: setting to the specified value. / GET: indicating the current value.
    -80.5 means muting. First decimal place must be always included. Ex: 10.0, -25.0 or 0.0 etc. The unit for this parameter is "dB".
